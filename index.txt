# about

About me

Been on Roblox for 4+ years. Started making complete games solo — maps, UI, economy, systems, all of it. At some point I realised the scripting side was what I actually came back to, so I went all in.

Now I focus purely on scripting and systems architecture. Because I've shipped full games before, I understand what a system needs before you explain it — I know what breaks in production, what players notice, and what's a shortcut vs what actually lasts.
